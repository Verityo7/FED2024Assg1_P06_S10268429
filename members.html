MORE MORE JUMP! Members

Hanasato Minori

Hanasato Minori (花里 みのり)

Gender: Female
Birthday: April 14 (♈︎)
Height: 159 cm (5'2")
School: Miyamasuzaka Girls Academy (Class 2-D)
Committee: Animal Care
Hobbies: Dance covers, Writing in her diary, Looking after animals
Specialty: Coming up with catchphrases
Favorite Food: Salmon
Least Favorite Food: Broccoli
Dislikes: Relaxing activities

Voice Actor: Ogura Yui

A wide-eyed hard worker. Minori was initially the only member of MORE MORE JUMP! with no prior idol experience, but her work with the group has led to her garnering a fanbase and performing in her first solo show. Now, she continues to work towards her goal of becoming an A-list idol worthy of performing at the Dome.

Background

When Minori was a child, she would often have bad luck. After one particular day, when she dropped the baton during a race at school, Minori stayed home out of guilt and fear that her classmates would be angry at her. She then saw Haruka from ASRUN, singing solo on TV. Minori was encouraged by Haruka's words that "Tomorrow will always be a better day", and has been a fan of Haruka ever since. Like her longtime inspiration, Minori also set her sights on becoming an idol. She has tried her hand at over 50 auditions, but failed every single one. Despite this, she never gave up and continued to try in order to achieve her dream.

While practicing on her school's roof, she met Haruka, who, along with Airi and Shizuku, helped her train to become a fully fledged idol. Thanks to Minori's positive and hardworking nature, she inspires hope in the other former idols and they form MORE MORE JUMP! together.

Minori has a younger brother who is currently in elementary school. She also owns a pet Samoyed named Samo-chan.


Personality

Minori is an optimistic, hard-working girl who has shown her determination and dedication to becoming an idol, something others often compliment her on. She has a kind heart and tries to be considerate of others. However, she is also clumsy, tends to get panicked easily, and often has trouble keeping her composure. This is accompanied by her habit of saying whatever's on her mind, and she seems to find it easy to express her emotions without restraint, purposefully or not. Despite her clumsiness, she is extremely disciplined, both in idol training and in her habit of daily writing.

She looks up to many of her friends and classmates and aspires to be like them. As she grows as both a person and an upcoming idol, she desires to bring the happiness that Haruka used to feel as an idol before retirement. Although it may not seem like so upon first meeting her, she is an empathetic and emotionally mature young girl. She has a habit of repeating the phrase "more and more" ("もっともっと", motto motto in Japanese).


Etymology

Hanasato (花里): Hana (花) means "flower" and Sato (里) means "village".

Minori (みのり) means “to bear fruit".

Kiritani Haruka

Kiritani Haruka (桐谷 遥)

Gender: Female
Birthday: October 5 (♎︎)
Height: 163 cm (5'4")
School: Miyamasuzaka Girls Academy (Class 2-D)
Hobbies: Collecting penguin-themed merch
Specialty: Basketball
Favorite Food: Anything sweet
Least Favorite Food: Wasabi
Dislikes: Idleness

Voice Actor: Yoshioka Mayu

Former member of ASRUN, an idol group known nationwide.The MORE MORE JUMP! solo show has left Haruka more ready than ever to pursue her idol career. Along with Minori, she has decided to switch over to the credit-based course and focus all her energy into being an idol once more.


Background

As a child, Haruka had a difficult time smiling even when she was happy. She was inspired to become an idol when, while at a mall, an idol invited Haruka to dance with her onstage, causing Haruka to smile. Haruka realized she wanted to become an idol upon hearing that the girl who invited her to dance was filled with hope by watching Haruka having fun. As a child and while in middle school, Haruka was an athlete, specifically a basketball player.

In third grade, Haruka decided she wanted to become an idol. She then became part of the nationally-acclaimed idol group ASRUN, where she ended up having the highest popularity ranking among all the members of the group. After a falling-out with one of the other members, she retired from being an idol and became a normal student at Miyamasuzaka Girls' Academy. Shortly after her retirement, she meets Minori on the rooftop while looking for a place to read. Soon, Minori, Airi, and Shizuku invite her to create MORE MORE JUMP! with them. She initially refuses, until she is given hope by former ASRUN member Mai.

Her mother is a nail stylist.


Personality

Haruka is very charismatic, formerly being the most popular member of ASRUN. In Minori's words, she is "the kind of idol who is able to give everyone hope for a better tomorrow". She is a strict and dedicated person, working in regular routines of rigorous workouts to stay at an idol's standard. However, due to her overly diligent nature, she has difficulty relaxing and giving herself a break. This dedication applies to her studies, as the VIRTUAL SINGERS mention her excellent grades. She restricts herself on sweets and pastries, which, in response, causes cravings. She is attentive to the schedules and specials of pastry shops.

Haruka is composed yet bold, unafraid to talk back and tease her friends. She is rather sporty, liking basketball, and as a child she was quite competitive. She also loves penguins, owns a ton of penguin merch, and sometimes gets emotional when receiving penguin merch as a gift.


Etymology

Kiritani (桐谷) means "paulownia", also known as "empress tree". In the language of flowers, paulownia means "noble".

Haruka (遥) means "far off, distant".

Momoi Airi

Momoi Airi (桃井 愛莉)

Gender: Female
Birthday: March 19 (♓︎)
Height: 156 cm (5'1")
School: Miyamasuzaka Girls Academy (Class 3-E)
Club: Tea Ceremony
Hobbies: Researching idols, Shopping
Specialty: Cooking, Looking after kids
Favorite Food: Japanese sweets
Least Favorite Food: Liver
Dislikes: Cats (being allergic)

Voice Actor: Furihata Ai

Former member of the idol group QT. She inspires the other members of MORE MORE JUMP! with her intense passion and pride for being an idol. Airi also excels at planning and scheduling, allowing her to proactively manage MORE MORE JUMP!'s work in the absence of an agency.


Background

Airi wanted to become an idol when she was young, as she admired the way they danced, sang and made people smile, and wanted to be able to do the same. At the same time, she wanted to be more girlish, as she was made fun of for being a tomboy and getting into fights with boys. Airi was mainly inspired by an idol named Rainbow Garland, who was also the first idol she became a fan of.

Airi later debuted as a member of the idol group QT. She was a popular idol that appeared on many variety shows. One of her most well-known TV personas was The Masked Variety Idol, Happy Everyday. Due to her talent working in television, her agency urged her to stop being an idol and to become a TV personality instead. Upon overhearing her manager outright saying that Airi is very average as an idol, she rejected this offer, resulting in her quitting the group.

Airi seemingly gave up her dream of being an idol. She even insulted Minori when they first met and said that she has no chance at becoming an idol. However, seeing Minori's spirit, she was reinvigorated by her determination to reach her dream, thus agreeing to train Minori and create MORE MORE JUMP! with Haruka and Airi's former rival Shizuku.

She is the middle child of her family, having one older sister and one younger sister. Her father is a good cook, as Airi mentions him making her and her sisters lunch and Christmas Cake.

Airi is part of the Miyamasuzaka credit course alongside Shizuku. As a second year, Airi was classmates with Shizuku in class 2-D. As a third year, she is classmates with Shizuku in class 3-E.


Personality

Airi is strong-minded and comes across as pushy to some. According to Minori, she is strict but reliable. She is very protective of her friends and family, and hates bullies. She was more tomboyish as a child, though that was frequently shot down by her mother. Airi retains some of her headstrong attitude, though cares greatly for her friends as well as her siblings, taking a sisterly role with both.

Before joining her unit, she would frequently think back on her past, bitter of how her agency ended up using her as a TV personality and disregarded her idol activities completely. When she would do this, she'd become snappy and irrational to those who would confront her about her attitude, insulting them, only to feel guilty afterwards.


Etymology

Momoi (桃井): Momo (桃) means "peach" and I (井) means "a well". In the language of flowers, the peach flower means "I am your captive", "invincible", or "charming". Momoi is also a place in Tokyo's Suginami Ward.

Airi (愛莉): Ai (愛) means "love" and Ri (莉) means "white jasmine". She is named after her mom's favorite actress.

Hinomori Shizuku

Hinomori Shizuku (日野森 雫)

Gender: Female
Birthday: December 6 (♐︎)
Height: 168 cm (5'6")
School: Miyamasuzaka Girls Academy (Class 3-E)
Committee: Health
Club: Archery
Hobbies: Reading, Taking walks
Specialty: Archery, Embroidery
Favorite Food: Udon noodles, Tofu skin
Least Favorite Food: Spicy foods
Dislikes: Reading maps, Using computers

Voice Actor: Honnizumi Rina

Former lead of the ever-popular idol group Cheerful * Days. Not getting along well with the other members, Shizuku ended up leaving the group with a heavy heart. However, a joint TV appearance with her old group gave her the chance to open up about the topic. With newfound courage, Shizuku is able to move on and move forward.


Background

Shizuku is the former leader of the idol group Cheerful＊Days, specifically its 3rd generation. Her friend applied to the position for her without Shizuku's knowledge, but she decided to go with it after passing their audition and being awarded as the "Grand Prix winner". She trained to become an idol at the same time as Airi, who openly viewed her as a rival.

Eventually, her agency forced her to act out the image of a "perfect idol", leading to her being known among the fans for her beautiful, mysterious, and mature appearance. However, this caused jealousy from the other members of her group, who were forced to constantly back her up, which eventually led to them thinking that she has no special talent and is only popular for her looks. Her groupmates' resentment towards Shizuku built up even more due to her hectic schedule and her inability to prioritize Cheerful＊Days' activities.

One day, Shizuku meets Minori on the school rooftop, who she agrees to help train along with Airi. However, after increasing tensions in her idol group and an argument with Airi, she decides to leave Cheerful＊Days. Minori and Airi give her the hope to be an idol again, and after performing a show for Haruka in the Stage SEKAI, the four of them create MORE MORE JUMP!

Her mother is a koto instructor, and her father used to be the guitarist of a famous band.

Shizuku is part of the Miyamasuzaka credit course alongside Airi. As a second year, Shizuku was classmates with Airi in class 2-D, as well as a member of the beautification committee and archery club. She joined the archery club after being recommended to do so by a senior member of Cheerful＊Days. As a third year, she is classmates with Airi in class 3-E, and a member of the health committee and archery club.


Personality

Shizuku has a kind and mature personality. She worries a lot about disappointing her fans, feeling she must maintain a "perfect" image, but after the event My Color! she begins to show her authentic self on live streams. While she appears very calm and poised, she does have some quirks, such as putting miso soup in her thermos and being extremely bad with technology. As she grows as an idol, she becomes determined to stay true to herself, not the perfect image she had before.

Shizuku can also be a bit air-headed, as according to her sister Shiho, she loves going on walks but can't remember any of the street names. As shown in multiple area conversations, she gets lost even in places familiar to her, such as the shopping mall.


Etymology

Hinomori (日野森) means "a sunny forest".

Shizuku (雫) means "droplet". She was named after the scenery her mother saw after she was born; it had just stopped raining, and the raindrops were sparkling in the sunlight.
